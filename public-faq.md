# Public FAQ: H1N1 Vaccine

## What is the H1N1 vaccine?
The H1N1 vaccine is a seasonal influenza vaccine that protects against the novel H1N1 strain.

## Who should get the vaccine?
The vaccine is recommended for all individuals aged 6 months and older, especially high-risk groups.

## Where can I get vaccinated?
Vaccination clinics are set up in Zones A, B, and C. Check the distribution plan for details.
